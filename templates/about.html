VAAK BHAAVNA

It is necessary to know the emotion behind a voice

Vaak-Bhaavna is a website-based application which tells us about emotions as well as gender of the speaker with the help of the speech input which the person gives and based on its speech-emotion recognition technique.It can classify emotion into 3 classes: Positive,Negative and Neutral. The emotions which can be classified into happy, sad, fear, angry, disgust, neutral and surprise.
